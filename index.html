A note from Sassy

This one meant everything.

I built Film Festival Atlas because I was tired of juggling screenshots, PDFs, and a hundred group-chat links just to figure out where I needed to be. I wanted something that worked the way I think — schedule at a glance, my connections in one place, no noise.

I did not expect 640 of you to find it. Out of roughly ten thousand people at that festival, one in sixteen walked through those doors and used something I built in my living room. That number is not small to me.

This was a beta — unpolished in places, held together with love and very late nights. You used it anyway. You added events, saved connections, planned your looks, wrote your notes. You let it be part of your festival experience. Thank you for that, genuinely.
