How to use
Film Festival Atlas.

A 7-minute walkthrough. Built for ABFF 2026 attendees who want a system, not another social app. Read top to bottom, or jump to whichever step you need.

01 · PIN 02 · Schedule 03 · Your Atlas 04 · Looks 05 · Connections 06 · QR Card 07 · Notes
01
Optional but recommended

Set your PIN

Keep your data private

Your atlas is private to your phone — nothing leaves your device. A 4-digit PIN keeps your itinerary, notes, and connections safe if you lose your phone.

Set it on the splash screen when you first open the app. You can change or remove it anytime from the Home tab → Change or remove PIN.

02
Five days, every event

Browse the schedule

Wed May 27 – Sun May 31

Hit the Schedule tab. Five day buttons — Wednesday through Sunday. Every screening, panel, party, and mixer the festival lists is here. Tap a day to dive into its timeline.

Tap Venue & Area Map at the top of the schedule to open the official festival map in your browser.

Always verify times and locations at abff.com — schedules change, especially in the final days before the festival.

03
Build your personal itinerary

Add events to your atlas

No FOMO required

On any day, tap + Add to my atlas on the events you actually want to attend. Your picks show up in My Atlas alongside the times and locations.

Skip what doesn't serve you. You can remove events the same way — tap the button again to toggle.

Pro move: Pre-load your whole week before Day 01. Edit down as you go — easier than scrambling mid-day.

04
Pin each outfit to an event

Plan your looks

Wake up knowing what to wear

Open the Looks tab. Tap + Add a look — give it a name, snap or upload a photo, drop a Pinterest link, then tag it to a specific event you've added.

Your looks show up alongside your itinerary so you're never scrambling the morning of. One look, one event. Done.

Best time to do this: The Sunday before the festival. Sit down, tag every outfit. Sleep is the only currency that matters at ABFF.

05
Names blur. Cards don't.

Log every connection

Five minutes before bed

Open the People tab. Tap + Log a new connection after every meaningful conversation — name, role, where you met, what you talked about.

Add a photo, social handles, website, and follow-up notes. Search and find them later when you need to send that email.

The ritual: Five minutes before bed each night, write down everyone you met that day. Future you will weep with gratitude.

06
Tap, scan, contact saved

Share your card via QR

No typing. No "what's your IG again."

Set up your card in the Me tab — name, role, handles, website, photo. When you meet someone, hit Share My Contact.

They point their camera at your screen. Your full contact card saves directly to their phone. One QR. One scan.

Set the tone: Share your card first before you ask for theirs. Removes friction, makes you memorable.

07
The festival brain dump

Notes + packing

Dump it all here

The Looks tab also has a Notes scratchpad for random thoughts, follow-ups, restaurant names, the bar where you almost cried — whatever needs to leave your head.

The Packing list is grouped by category: Wardrobe, Tech, Beauty, Documents. Tick items off as you pack.
