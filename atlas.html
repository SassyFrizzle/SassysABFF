About this app
⌄
What is this?
Film Festival Atlas is a personal planning tool for film festivals — built using ABFF 2026 as the pilot. Browse the schedule, build your personal itinerary, log the people you meet, plan your looks, jot notes, and check off your packing list.
What does it not do?
It does not sell tickets, register you for events, or have any official connection to ABFF. Always verify event times and locations at abff.com.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Everything you enter stays on your device in your browser's local storage. Nothing is sent to any server. Nobody else can see your data, including the creator of this app.
Is it an app I download?
No — it's a web app that runs in your browser. On iPhone, tap the share icon in Safari and choose "Add to Home Screen." On Android, tap the three-dot menu and choose "Add to Home Screen."
What if I clear my browser or switch devices?
Your data lives in your browser's local storage. Clearing your browser data or opening the link on a different device will start you fresh — there is no account or cloud sync.
Who made this?
Nicole "Sassy" Harrison (@sassyfrizzle) — filmmaker, producer, creative director, and the person who needed a better way to move through a film festival.
Is this affiliated with ABFF?
No. Film Festival Atlas is an independent tool. Not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by ABFF, ABFF Ventures LLC, or any of their partners.
